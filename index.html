Featured Work

Bundaberg

Bundaberg Brewed Drinks, originating from Australia, has been crafting sparkling beverages since 1968. They wanted to deepen consumer perception of their brewing craftsmanship, vibrant aesthetics, and ease of drink mixing through social media to create a high-end image for their beverages.

Antipodes

Antipodes, a premium water brand from New Zealand aimed to shape its image in China around minimalist aesthetics and attract new consumers. We leveraged influencers (KOL), content marketing, and paid ad strategies to establish Antipodes as a distinctive lifestyle brand.

Louis Vuitton

For Valentine's Day, Louis Vuitton launched a new line of couple's perfumes and approach us to promote the new fragrance through influencer marketing.

Dior

In conjunction with the 520 Valentine's Day campaign in 2023, Dior sought to enhance the market presence of its couple's fragrance line. The objective was to leveraging influencer marketing to promote the perfumes to couples and bolster sales performance.

Samyang Ramen

Samyang, is a South Korean instant noodle brand best known for their Spicy Chicken Flavor Noodles, commonly referred to as "Buldak." The brand wanted to promote their flagship line through short Douyin (TikTok) videos that showcased the noodles in everyday life scenarios to drive direct sales.

Frescobaldi

Frescobaldi is one of Italy's most renowned and historic wine producers, with a rich history dating back over 700 years. The brand has both online and offline distribution in China. We were approached to managed their social media campaigns and WeChat ads to increase reach to their target audience in specific regions.
